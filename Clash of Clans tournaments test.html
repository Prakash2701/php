CLASH OF CLANS WORLD CHAMPIONSHIP

The Clash of Clans World Championships is Supercell’s official esports program, featuring the best
Clash of Clans players and teams out there. The World Championships aims to give players, teams
and spectators the most exciting and entertaining competition in the world.



Since 2019 the program has established itself as the highest form of competitive Clash out there.
2023 will keep being true to the foundation we’ve set with our community, and Clash is here to
